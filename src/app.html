Land Change Projections for Hawai'i

Model Description and Summary

The Hawaii model was developed by the USGS LUCC team using the LUCAS model. The model was run over two scenarios, for 50 annual timesteps for years between 2011 and 2061, over 10 interations. Results are summarized by island and moisture zone.
